Greedy Dog

There was a dog that was very hungry. He searched for food every where and at last he found a bond with his mouth and started going back to his home.There was a bridge on the way to his home. When he started crossing it he saw his reflection in the water.

The dog thought that there was another dog with a bone in water. He want to get that other bone too. So he starting barking at his own reflection. His bone fell in water as soon as he opened his mouth. The greedy dog lost his bone.

Moral:Greedy is a cure.
