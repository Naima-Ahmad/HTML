Thirsty Crow

Once there was a crow flying in the sky. It was hot summer and he felt thirsty. He looked for water here and there. But he could not find any water.

At last he saw a pot with a little water at the bottom of it. He tried, but could not reach the water. He got an idea to rise up the water level. So, he picked up the small stones nearby and dropped them into the pot one by one. Now the water rose up. He drink water and flew away happily.

Moral : We should use our common sense to acheive our goal.
